I am an MBA candidate with a strong interest in business analytics, strategy, and data-driven decision-making. My academic and practical work focuses on understanding how organizations use data to improve performance, manage uncertainty, and make informed strategic choices.

I approach business problems by first structuring them clearly, identifying key variables, and defining measurable outcomes. I then analyze data to uncover insights that can guide managerial decisions rather than just describe trends.

In addition to analytics, I have hands-on experience in website development. I view technical skills as an execution layer that supports business thinking, allowing me to prototype ideas, build dashboards, and understand digital systems from an operational perspective.

My long-term goal is to work in roles that combine analytics, strategy, and technology to help organizations make better decisions and adapt to changing business environments.
