Business Performance Dashboard

A business analytics project designed to support managerial decision-making through clear and actionable performance metrics.

Back to Projects

Business Context

Growing businesses generate large volumes of operational and sales data. Managers often struggle to convert this data into insights that can guide timely and effective decisions. Dashboards play a critical role in bridging this gap.

Problem Statement

The objective of this project was to design a performance dashboard that provides management with a clear overview of revenue trends, customer metrics, and operational performance without overwhelming them with data.

Approach & Methodology

  • Identified key performance indicators relevant to management decisions
  • Cleaned and structured raw business data for analysis
  • Designed visualizations focused on clarity and usability
  • Ensured insights were easy to interpret for non-technical users

Tools Used

  • Excel / Google Sheets
  • Power BI / Tableau
  • Data visualization best practices

Key Insights

  • Clear visibility into monthly and quarterly revenue trends
  • Identification of high-performing and underperforming segments
  • Improved understanding of customer acquisition and retention patterns
  • Early detection of performance bottlenecks

Business Impact

The dashboard enables faster and more informed decision-making by presenting critical metrics in a structured and intuitive format. It supports management reviews, performance monitoring, and strategic planning.
